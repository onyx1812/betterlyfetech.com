Listen to the Doctor

One of the nation’s top podiatrists who works with thousands of people a year had some grave warnings:

A recent study shows 20% of Americans have serious foot pain, and that the number for women is much higher. Making matters worse, 8% of all adults (that's 16 million people) suffer from long-term back pain. So many people don’t understand the back is directly tied to the feet!

Your feet have more than 100 muscles, but you don't use all those muscles while walking or standing. When people walk the wrong way, when your feet are wrongly aligned, your knees and hips follow and then your back falls into misalignment.

This contributes to lower-back pain; so, oddly enough, injured, hurting feet are directly related to an injured, hurting back.
