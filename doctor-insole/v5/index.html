How Many Are Suffering?

According to the National Foot Health Assessment, about 18 million of U.S. adults have flat feet. Another 8 million people, have fallen arches. Flat feet and fallen arches are a common condition which occurs when the arches don't develop during childhood. In other cases, flatfeet develop after an injury or from the simple wear-and-tear stresses of age.

Flat feet contribute to problems in your ankles and knees because the condition can alter the alignment of your legs. They also cause chronic foot pain, which is unbearable for millions of Americans.
