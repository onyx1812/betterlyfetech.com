Foot Pain Is Complicated

The human foot has 26 bones and 33 joints, and the older we get there is a greater chance for a variety of issues. Foot pain affects roughly one in four people over 65. For older women especially, studies show there are major risk factors for the development of foot pain.

These foot pains can be caused by pinched toes, bunions, plantar fasciitis, weak ankles or any other number of issues.
