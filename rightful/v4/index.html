3 things seniors do to destroy their Knees (and one they swear by)

by Eden Bidani
Beauty and Lifestyle

Grandma Jan once told me she loved to go dancing in high heels. Her stories always made me feel like I was right there with her.

Her dancing days were long gone and all those high heels she wore as a kid really took a toll on her knees in general.

She said she spoke to her doctor and he told her there were three main ways seniors are destroying their knees.

  1. Overuse – This sounds weird right? Like, how can you overuse your knees? Too much walking? But seriously, it’s a problem. And not just overuse, but poor use like wearing high heels when it’s time to just move on from those.
  2. Previous Injuries – All my grandparents have a hard time admitting they might need to do more than ice their knees. Sports injuries, war wounds and more can contribute to knee pain.
  3. Overweight – A recent study showed that more than 35% of people over 65 years old have an unhealthy weight. It’s just awful, and what’s worse is the solution to one is a problem for another!

You can walk all you want, but if your knees hurt then it will only make overuse worse.
